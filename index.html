01 — About

A bit about me

I'm a Computer Science Engineering undergraduate at Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology, Thiruvananthapuram. I enjoy the full arc of building software — from sketching the interface to shipping a performant, well-structured product.

My focus is the modern web: writing semantic, accessible markup, designing with intent, and keeping things fast. I care about details — the spacing, the easing curve, the empty state — because they're what make a product feel trustworthy.

Outside coursework, I'm usually deep in a side project or learning a new tool. When I step away from the screen, you'll find me chasing aerials in Rocket League, listening to music, or solving puzzles — they keep my reflexes quick, my game sense sharp, and my problem-solving instincts fresh.
